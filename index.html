Privacy Policy for ScrubDex

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

ScrubDex is a surgical case logging and journaling application designed for personal record-keeping, self-audit, and workflow organization. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

1. Data Collection

ScrubDex does not collect, transmit, or store user-entered case data on developer-controlled servers.

  • No personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number) is collected by the developer
  • No analytics services are integrated
  • No crash reporting services are integrated
  • No advertising SDKs or tracking technologies are used
  • No data is sold, rented, profiled, or monetized

2. User-Entered Data

User-entered data may include case logs, diagnoses, procedures, patient-related notes, tasks, and images.

  • All such data is stored locally on the user’s device
  • It is not transmitted to the developer
  • It is not stored on ScrubDex-controlled servers
  • The developer does not access or process this data

3. Permissions and Device Features

Biometric Authentication

ScrubDex may use device-level biometric authentication (such as fingerprint or facial recognition) to secure access to the app.

  • Biometric authentication is processed entirely by the device’s operating system
  • ScrubDex does not collect, store, or access biometric data
  • The app receives only confirmation of successful authentication

Camera Access

ScrubDex may request access to the device camera to allow users to capture and attach images to case entries.

  • Captured images are stored locally on the device
  • Images are not transmitted to the developer
  • Users may revoke camera permission at any time in device settings

Storage Access

Storage access may be required to save exported files or create local backups.

Notification Permission

Notification permission may be requested to enable reminders and scheduled alerts.

4. Data Storage and Processing

Local Storage

  • All user-entered data remains stored locally on the device
  • ScrubDex does not operate backend servers for case storage

Optional Google Drive Backup

ScrubDex provides an optional, user-initiated Google Drive backup feature.

  • Backup is manually initiated by the user
  • Data is stored within the user’s own Google Drive account
  • Data transfer occurs directly between the app and Google Drive
  • ScrubDex does not store backup copies on developer servers
  • The developer does not access Google Drive backup data

Use of Google Drive is governed by Google’s own privacy policies.

5. Data Export

ScrubDex allows users to export data in PDF, JSON, or CSV format.

  • Export files are generated entirely on the device
  • Exported files are saved or shared at the user’s discretion
  • Exported data is not transmitted to the developer

Users are solely responsible for ensuring exported files are handled in compliance with applicable institutional policies and patient confidentiality obligations.

6. Data Sharing

ScrubDex does not sell, rent, or share user-entered data with third parties.

If Google Drive backup is enabled, data is shared only with Google Drive under the user’s control and subject to Google’s privacy terms.

7. Medical Disclaimer

ScrubDex is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

  • The app is intended solely for logging and organizational purposes
  • It does not replace professional medical judgment or institutional records

Users are solely responsible for how recorded information is used.

8. User Control and Data Deletion

  • Entries may be added, edited, or deleted within the app
  • Google Drive backups may be enabled, disabled, or deleted by the user
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes locally stored data

The developer cannot access or recover deleted data.

9. Children’s Privacy

ScrubDex is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if features or legal requirements change. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date.
